Nemo

Nemo is about a a young, adventurous, and mighty clown fish that lives with his very cautious father Marlin.
Nemo has a foreshortened fin and gets tired of those around him underestimating him and swims too close to the surface
to prove himself. In the process he is caught by a diver. His father and an angel fish named Dory, with short term memory
loss, set out to find him.

Why this is my Favorite Movie:

  • Nemo reminded me of myself as a child. Growing up with a fin that did not work as well as the other one really motivated me as a child.
  • Eventhough Nemo did have a fin smaller than the other he did not let that stop him from doing what he wanted.
  • My sun sign is also in Pisces and that just explains everything else.
